About Our Project

Our project is an AI-powered digital health assistant designed to make disease diagnosis and treatment support more accessible and reliable. By analyzing patient symptoms, the system predicts the most likely conditions and suggests safe, guideline-based medications along with correct dosages. It also provides precautions, side effects, and confidence levels to ensure transparency.

The solution empowers patients and clinicians in regions with limited medical access, while ensuring safety by flagging critical cases for professional review. With natural language input, explainable AI, our assistant aims to improve healthcare access and reduce diagnostic delays.
